A single page introduction to prayer

From intention, to position
From recitation to dua.

There's too much empty space, how do I fix this?

Intention

Niyyah

Make the intention to pray.

Position

Takbir, Qiyam, Ruku, Sujud, Tashahud, Taslim

Move into the appropriate stance, initially standing, then bowing, kneeling and prostrating.

Recitation

Takbir, Thanka, Thaawudh, Fatihah, Ikhlas, Tasbeeh, Tashahud, Salawat,

As you move into each stance, recite the appropriate prayer.
